About Us

"To Achieve and maintain a high level of quality, which translates company reputations with clients"


Ransom Mary Limited, incorporated in London, is one of the providers of IT solutions. We provide value added LAN / WAN Networking products, Telecommunication, Security Solutions, Data centre Solutions and Office automation. We represent major brands in each respective field. The company's philosophy is aimed at total customers satisfaction in all spheres, with long view for the future. Ransom Mary promises excellent services and products.

We at Ransom Mary Limited, understand the dynamics of the business and enables clients, transform themselves to embrace the future . Represent leading brands in the industry, we are happy to introduce our product and service to your organization. Ransom Mary has proven competence in structured cabling (Fiber/Copper/Telecom), Wireless Solutions- Indoor/Outdoor,Telephone Systems(PABX) and Security Surveillance System (CCTV and public address systems). We also supply all major brands of Servers, Desktops, Laptops and other IT equipments.

Our mission is to understand and meet the needs of our clients by delivering quality value added solutions.

  • Desktops, Laptops and Servers
  • Wireless Solutions
  • CCTV and public address systems
  • Structured Cabling for Voice and Data
  • Low current and fire alarm cables
  • Fiber Optic Cabling Solutions
  • Email Hosting and Web Solutions
  • Tele Communication Systems
